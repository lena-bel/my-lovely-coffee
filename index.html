Our Story

La Boîte à Café was born from a simple passion: bringing exceptional coffee experiences to the heart of Lyon. Since opening our doors, we've been dedicated to creating a welcoming space where coffee lovers can discover carefully crafted beverages and delicious brunch options.

Our philosophy is simple — quality over quantity. We work directly with coffee farmers to ensure ethical sourcing and the freshest beans possible. Every cup tells a story of dedication, from bean to cup.

Whether you're looking for your morning espresso, a peaceful workspace, or a cozy brunch with friends, La Boîte à Café is your home away from home.
